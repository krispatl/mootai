FAQ

Do we need to install anything?

No. It’s browser-native. Share a link, pick roles, and start.

Can we use our own rubric?

Yes. Upload your criteria or start from our templates and adjust weights.

Does the AI grade for us?

It provides consistent rubric scores and explanations. Instructors remain in control.

How is data handled?

Rooms are invite-only. Transcripts/recordings are opt-in with time-boxed retention options.
